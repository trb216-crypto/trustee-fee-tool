Relevant Case Law:
In re McKinney, 383 B.R. 490 (Bankr. N.D. Cal. 2008): Court emphasized strict application of statutory maximums under 11 U.S.C. ยง 326(a). Trustee compensation must adhere to the statutory tier system unless specifically adjusted by the court.

In re Biskup, 236 B.R. 332 (Bankr. N.D. Ohio 1999): Courts have discretion to award less than the statutory maximum, but not more. Actual services rendered and benefit to the estate are relevant in awarding final compensation.
